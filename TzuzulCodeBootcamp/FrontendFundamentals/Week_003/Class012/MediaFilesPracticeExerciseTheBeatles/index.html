Get Back

Documentary about The Beatles with numerous unpublished material that shows the cordiality, camaraderie and humor that reigned during the making of "Let It Be", the legendary Beatles studio album, and their last live concert as a group, the unforgettable Savile Row rooftop performance in London. More than 55 hours of previously unreleased material, filmed by Michael Lindsay-Hogg in 1969, and 140 hours of rarely heard audio recordings of the "Let It Be" album recording sessions have been collected. In addition, Peter Jackson has worked in concert with the living beatles and the widows of the deceased, having access to numerous material that sees the light for the first time.
